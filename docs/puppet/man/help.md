---
title: "Man Page: puppet help"
---


USAGE: puppet help *action*

Display Puppet help.

OPTIONS: --render-as FORMAT - The rendering format to use. --verbose - Whether to log verbosely. --debug - Whether to log debug information.

ACTIONS: help Display help about Puppet subcommands and their actions.

See 'puppet help help' or 'man puppet-help' for full help.
